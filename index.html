Battletech Master Unit List (MUL) Visualizer

This dashboard gives a high-level overview of the differences in the mechs fielded by the great houses during the lostech era of the Succession Wars (2901-3019).

Note that generally available IS mechs are not shown in order to highlight unique faction tendencies. This is currently a prototype and I'm aiming to expand it further.

Filter by clicking on a House's weight class or role to get a more in-depth breakdown by weight or role.
